Welcome to the Computer Science and Engineering Branch

At our CSE branch, we are committed to providing cutting-edge education in computer science and engineering. Our programs prepare students to tackle real-world challenges across various domains, from software development to networking and beyond.

Discover innovative research opportunities, hands-on learning experiences, and a supportive community dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology.

Computer Science and Engineering is foundational to modern technology. CSE professionals contribute to advancements in software development, networking, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, data analytics, and more, shaping the future of global industries.

B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering started at SRKREC with an intake of 120 seats. This program aims to equip graduates with strong theoretical foundations and practical skills in computer science, including software development, algorithms, data structures, networking, cybersecurity, and cloud computing. It also emphasizes communication skills, teamwork, and ethical practices essential for a successful career in CSE.

The department collaborates with industry leaders to offer workshops, seminars, and internships that enhance practical knowledge and employability. Advanced infrastructure and cloud resources are available to both faculty and students through partnerships with industry experts.
