Welcome to the Information Technology Branch

At our IT branch, we are committed to providing comprehensive education in information technology. Our programs prepare students to excel in various domains, from software development to network administration and beyond.

Discover opportunities for hands-on learning, innovative research, and community engagement, essential for navigating the dynamic field of IT.

Information Technology plays a crucial role in modern businesses and industries. IT professionals drive technological advancements, manage networks, ensure cybersecurity, and leverage data analytics to support organizational success.

B.Tech. in Information Technology at SRKREC offers a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical skills. Students gain expertise in software engineering, database management, network security, cloud computing, and more, preparing them for diverse career paths in IT.

The department collaborates with industry leaders to provide workshops, seminars, and internships, enhancing practical skills and industry readiness. Advanced computing resources and infrastructure support both learning and research activities.

Career Opportunities:
Graduates from the Information Technology branch can pursue roles such as:
a. Software Developer
b. Network Administrator
c. Cybersecurity Specialist
d. Data Analyst
e. System Architect
f. Web Developer
g. IT Consultant
h. Database Manager...
